Hi, I'm Annie Linner

A web developer with SEO expertise

Glad you're here!

I'm a passionate and driven aspiring front-end developer. I love creating beautiful, modern and fully responsive websites. I also have a lot of SEO experience. Combining SEO with my front-end skills is my strength.

I've spent the last 3 years in Malta, challenging myself in the fast-paced and demanding iGaming business. In November 2019 I will be moving back home to Sweden, to settle down in beautiful Växjö. Apart from coding, my biggest passion in life is being in nature and caring for the environment. Therefore, I think Växjö is the place for me.
